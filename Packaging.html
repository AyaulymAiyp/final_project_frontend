Packaging

The Prada Group is committed to increasing the usage of a more sustainable packaging.


Paper consumption for packaging

Paper consumption for packaging In 2021, 89% of the total paper for packaging consumption was certified by the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) or recycled.


Plastic consumption for packaging

In recent years, the Group embarked on a program within the clothing and leather goods divisions aimed at converting some packaging components made from 100% virgin plastic into recycled or regenerated plastic. In 2021, plastic with recycled content reached 61% out of a total of 500 tons (49% in 2020).
